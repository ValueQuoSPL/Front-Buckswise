Upload Statement

    We can fetch your Mutual Fund details from the Email ID registered against your investment account in 4 easy steps

  • Open the CAMS investor services link here and click on "Click Here" link present under the title "CAS - CAMS, KARVY, FT, SBFS". (There is no direct access provided to the consolidated statement page; hence, this is the shortest way to fetch the PDF statement).
  • Follow the simple steps mentioned on the page to receive your consolidated mutual fund account statement to your email. Please ensure that you choose "Summary" under Statement Type.
  • Open your email account and download the PDF received.
  • Upload the document by clicking on "SELECT PDF" below and enter the password that you had entered while filling the details on CAMS.

    • Note: Since the file you downloaded from CAMS is password protected, we require the password to read the file. We can assure you that we don't save the passwords you enter to open this file.

    If you have mutual fund accounts registered with more than 1 email address, please upload all the statements.
